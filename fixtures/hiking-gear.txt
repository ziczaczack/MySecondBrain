Weekend hiking gear checklist — boots, layers, and water for a two-day trip

Break in waterproof boots well before the trip to avoid blisters on the trail.
Pack moisture-wicking base layers, an insulating mid-layer, and a packable rain shell for changing weather.
Carry at least two liters of water plus a filter or purification tablets for refilling at streams.
Bring a headlamp, a basic first-aid kit, a map, and a compass even if you plan to use your phone.
High-energy snacks like trail mix and jerky keep your pace steady on long climbs.
Tell someone your route and expected return time before heading out.
