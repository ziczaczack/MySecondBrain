# Personal income tax filing — deadlines and documents checklist

The federal return is generally due April 15; if it falls on a weekend it shifts to the next business day.
Request an extension with Form 4868 to push the filing deadline to October 15, but pay any tax owed by April.
Gather W-2s from employers and 1099 forms for freelance, interest, and dividend income before starting.
Keep receipts for deductible expenses: charitable donations, mortgage interest, and medical costs over the threshold.
Quarterly estimated payments are due in April, June, September, and January for self-employed income.
File electronically for faster refunds and keep a copy of the return for at least three years.
